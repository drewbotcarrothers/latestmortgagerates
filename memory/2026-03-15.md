## Mortgage Calculator Math Fix

**Problem:** "Total Paid (5yr)" was showing incorrect values - it was calculating totals over the full amortization period (25 years) instead of just the 5-year term.

**Root Cause:** The `calculateMortgage` function was returning cumulative totals from the entire schedule (until mortgage payoff) rather than summing only the payments during the term period.

**Fix:** Added term-specific calculations that sum only the payments within the term period:
```typescript
const termTotalPaid = termSchedule.reduce((sum, entry) => sum + entry.payment, 0);
const termTotalInterest = termSchedule.reduce((sum, entry) => sum + entry.interestPayment, 0);
const termPrincipalPaid = mortgageAmount - termSchedule[termSchedule.length - 1].balance;
const termRemainingBalance = termSchedule[termSchedule.length - 1].balance;
```

**Result:** Now correctly shows:
- Total Paid (5yr) = Only payments made during the 5-year term
- Principal Paid = Principal reduction during the 5-year term  
- Interest Paid = Interest paid during the 5-year term
- Remaining Balance = Balance at end of the 5-year term

**Commit:** `7eac0f8`
**Status:** ✅ Fixed and deployed
