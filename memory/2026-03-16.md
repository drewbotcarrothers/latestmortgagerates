# Mortgage Rates Project - March 16, 2026

## New Calculators Added

Created and deployed three new calculator tools for the mortgage rates site:

### 1. Rent vs Buy Calculator
- **Location**: `app/components/RentVsBuyCalculator.tsx`
- **Page**: `app/tools/rent-vs-buy-calculator/page.tsx`
- **Features**:
  - Compares renting vs buying over time
  - Calculates break-even point
  - Shows net worth projections for both scenarios
  - Factors in home appreciation and investment returns
  - Adjustable time period (5-25 years)

### 2. Refinance Calculator
- **Location**: `app/components/RefinanceCalculator.tsx`
- **Page**: `app/tools/refinance-calculator/page.tsx`
- **Features**:
  - Estimates mortgage penalties (3-month interest or IRD)
  - Calculates break-even point
  - Shows monthly savings and total interest savings
  - Determines if refinancing makes financial sense
  - Includes closing costs in calculations

### 3. Closing Costs Calculator
- **Location**: `app/components/ClosingCostsCalculator.tsx`
- **Page**: `app/tools/closing-costs-calculator/page.tsx`
- **Features**:
  - Calculates total cash needed to close on a home
  - Province-specific land transfer taxes (all 10 provinces)
  - First-time buyer rebates (Ontario, BC, PEI)
  - Toronto municipal LTT option
  - Includes legal fees, inspection, appraisal, title insurance, moving costs
