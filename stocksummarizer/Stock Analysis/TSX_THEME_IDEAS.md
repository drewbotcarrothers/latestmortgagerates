## 📋 SELECTION CRITERIA TEMPLATES

For each theme, you can use these criteria combinations:

**Income Focus:**
- Dividend yield > X%
- Payout ratio < 75%
- X+ years consecutive payments
- Dividend growth > inflation

**Quality Focus:**
- ROE > 15%
- Debt/Equity < 1
- Operating margin > 15%
- Positive FCF

**Value Focus:**
- P/E < 12 or sector average
- P/B < 2
- EV/EBITDA < 8
- FCF yield > 8%

**Growth Focus:**
- Revenue growth > 15% YoY
- EPS growth > 20% YoY
- Expanding margins
- Market share gains

**Contrarian Focus:**
- 1-year performance < -20%
- Insider buying
- Below 52-week high
- Turnaround catalyst
