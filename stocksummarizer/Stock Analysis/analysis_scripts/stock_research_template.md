## Pro Tips

**Efficient Searching:**
- Use date filters (Tools → Recent → Past 3 months)
- Check "News" tab specifically for headlines
- Use site:globeandmail.com or site:bloomberg.com for quality sources
- Search investor relations directly: "[company] investor relations"

**Credibility:**
- Prefer primary sources (company disclosures) over opinion pieces
- Check dates - ensure news is recent
- Cross-reference claims across multiple sources
- Note analyst conflicts of interest (if they have price targets)

**Balance:**
- Don't cherry-pick only positive or only negative news
- Steel-man the opposing view (make strongest case against your thesis)
- Acknowledge uncertainty ("this could go either way if...")
- Update your view if evidence contradicts initial thesis
