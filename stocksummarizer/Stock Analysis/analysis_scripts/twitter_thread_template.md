- [ ] No "buy/sell" language

## ENGAGEMENT OPTIMIZATION

**Best practices:**
- Post between 8-10 AM EST on weekdays
- Reply to first comments within 30 minutes
- Pin thread to profile for 24 hours
- Share 1-2 individual insights as standalone tweets next day
- Track which charts get most engagement (note for future)

**Avoid:**
- Posting during market hours (people busy trading)
- Weekend posts (lower engagement)
- Defensive replies to criticism
- Arguing about individual stock picks
